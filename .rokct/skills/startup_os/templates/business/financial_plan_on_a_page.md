## 3. Cost Control & Mitigation Plan
1.  **Infrastructure Elasticity**: Docker-based hosting scales dynamically, ensuring that server costs mirror actual user transaction volume.
2.  **Gig-Fleet Flexing**: Delivery commissions are paid purely per successful delivery, turning a historically massive fixed delivery cost into a strictly variable cost.
3.  **Local Supply Sourcing**: Sourcing raw materials or technology from certified local vendors reduces currency and cross-border shipping risks.
